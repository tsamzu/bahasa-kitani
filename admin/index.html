How to Use

Managing your dictionary entirely from your phone.

📱 No computer needed. Everything here works from your phone browser. The only tool you need is your GitHub account and this admin page.
1
Adding a new word
Go to Add Word tab → fill in the fields → tap Generate Code → tap Copy Code.

Then tap GitHub ↗ — it opens your data file directly. Tap the pencil ✏️ to edit. Scroll to the very bottom, find the line that just says ];, tap the line above it, and paste. Then scroll down and tap Commit changes. Your word is live in 1–2 minutes.
2
Changing the password
Open admin/index.html on GitHub, tap edit ✏️, and find:

const ADMIN_PASSWORD = '...';

Change it to your new password and commit. Write it down somewhere safe.
3
Managing feedback
When a visitor submits feedback on your site, their email app opens addressed to you. When you receive the email, come to the Feedback tab, paste the message, and add it. Mark entries as Resolved once you've acted on them. Data is saved in your browser.
4
Adding phrases
Phrases live in the PHRASES array in data/tutong.js. Open it on GitHub, tap edit ✏️, and copy the format of an existing phrase. Fields are: id, tutong, malay, english, cat.
